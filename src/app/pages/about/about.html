Faariz Haider Sheikh

About Me

I'm Faariz Haider Sheikh — a Canadian web and game developer, and writer currently studying Computer Programming at St. Clair College. I enjoy combining logic, design, and storytelling through my projects, whether it's building digital experiences or creating immersive worlds.

Early Life and Education

I was born on November 23, 2005, in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan, and raised in Canada after my family moved when I was about six years old. I attended Aden Bowman Collegiate in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, from grades 9 to 11, and completed grade 12 at Tecumseh Vista Academy in Windsor, Ontario, earning my high school diploma. I'm now pursuing a Computer Programming diploma at St. Clair College, where I continue learning and developing new skills across multiple programming languages and development platforms.

Career

Game Development

My journey in game development began with Unity, where I learned to build interactive 2D and 3D experiences. I've completed several small Unity Learn projects, including Robo-Jump: I See Stars, a 2D platformer about collecting stars, and Unity Essentials, which features multiple mini-games accessible through a main menu. I'm slowly beginning to focus on creating original titles, blending multiple genres like mystery, atmosphere, and psychological horror.

Web Development

In web development, I work with both front-end and back-end technologies, using languages such as CSS, HTML, JS, JSON, PHP, SASS, SCSS, SQL. My focus is on building responsive, accessible, and user-friendly websites — combining visual design with efficient functionality. I also handle server-side validation and database integration for secure and dynamic projects.

Writing

Beyond coding, I'm passionate about writing fantasy, fiction, and horror. I share my work on Amazon KDP where I self-publish books. My stories often explore deep emotions, moral struggles, and the resilience of the human spirit. I also write articles on Medium, like issues related to the internet or media. Through writing, I aim to connect with readers the same way I connect with players and users — through immersive storytelling and meaningful experiences.
